# Business Analyst

## Overview
The Business Analyst is responsible for identifying, analyzing, and documenting business requirements and translating them into actionable tasks for technical teams. They serve as the bridge between stakeholders and the development team to ensure alignment of project goals and deliverables.

## Key Responsibilities
- Gather and clarify business requirements from stakeholders
- Analyze processes for efficiencies, risks, and gaps
- Translate requirements to user stories or technical specifications
- Collaborate closely with Product Managers, engineering, QA, and Stakeholder Liaison
- Support UAT and ensure delivered solutions meet business needs

## Typical Interactions
- Stakeholder Liaison: For requirement validation and feedback loop coordination
- Product Manager: For prioritization and alignment of business goals
- Engineers: For clear communication of technical requirements and hand-off

# Scrum Master

## Overview
The Scrum Master facilitates Agile processes, conducts ceremonies, removes bottlenecks for the team, and fosters continuous improvement and collaboration.

## Key Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint planning, stand-ups, and retrospectives
- Remove blockers for the team
- Coach the team on Agile best practices
- Foster open communication and feedback

## Typical Interactions
- Project Team: For sprint coordination and support
- Product Owner: For sprint goals and backlog management
- Stakeholders: For process transparency

# Stakeholder Liaison

## Overview
The Stakeholder Liaison acts as the primary contact for high-level project stakeholders, ensuring communication channels remain open and feedback is regularly solicited and integrated into project planning and delivery.

## Key Responsibilities
- Maintain relationships with project stakeholders
- Gather and relay feedback to the project team
- Facilitate regular touchpoints and communications
- Communicate project risks, changes, and status

## Typical Interactions
- Business Analyst: For capturing detailed business requirements
- Product Manager: For strategic updates and stakeholder priorities
- Project Manager: For alignment and reporting
